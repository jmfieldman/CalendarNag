# CalendarNag

### A macOS menu bar app for people who keep missing meetings they knew about.

[Download latest version](https://github.com/jmfieldman/CalendarNag/releases/latest)

---

## Why I made this

I kept missing meetings. Not because I didn't know about them. I always knew about them. That's the dumb part.

Here's what would happen. Ten minutes before a meeting, macOS would pop a little notification in the corner. I'd look at it, go "yep, got it," swipe it away, and go back to what I was doing. Then I'd be deep in a pull request or a bug or a Slack thread, totally absorbed.

> **Are you joining?**

Meeting started five minutes ago. Everyone's sitting there. Facepalm.

The issue isn't that I didn't *see* the reminder. I saw it. The issue is that I saw it once, at a moment when the meeting still felt far away, dismissed it, and then my brain filed it under "handled" and moved on. A notification you can swipe away in half a second is barely a reminder at all when you're the kind of person who gets absorbed in things.

I needed something that would keep nagging me. Something that got louder the closer the meeting got. Something I couldn't just dismiss and forget about.

So I built it.
